What is the Fire Weather Index?

The FWI System is a comprehensive model that assesses fire danger based on meteorological data. It's a critical tool for fire managers worldwide.

Fuel Moisture Codes

Represents the moisture content of forest floor fuels of different sizes, crucial for ignition potential.

Fire Behavior Indexes

Indicates the potential rate of fire spread and the amount of fuel available for combustion.

The Final FWI Value

A single numerical rating of fire intensity, combining all components to provide a clear danger assessment.
